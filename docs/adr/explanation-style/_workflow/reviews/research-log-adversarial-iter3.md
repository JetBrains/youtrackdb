### A16 [suggestion]
**Certificate**: C19 (Assumption test: the fenced append literal composes cleanly at the 11 sites)
**Target**: S1 — the 11-site append spec "just append a fifth list item" plus the fenced literal `, and `## Orientation`` (`research-log.md:333-338`)

One find/replace per site keeps the copy-bytes-not-intent contract and lands grammatical output.
